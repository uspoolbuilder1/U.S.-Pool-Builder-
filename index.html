About Us

Welcome to U.S. Pool Builder, your trusted pool construction company in Katy, Texas. We specialize exclusively in premium inground gunite concrete swimming pools designed to match your vision, property, and lifestyle.


Our team focuses on quality craftsmanship, durable construction, and exceptional customer service. Whether you're planning a modern luxury pool or a family-friendly backyard oasis, we bring your dream pool to life.
