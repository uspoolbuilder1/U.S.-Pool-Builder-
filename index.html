About Us

Welcome to U.S. Pool Builder, your trusted partner for professional pool construction in Katy, Texas. We specialize in building high-quality inground gunite concrete pools that are designed for durability, beauty, and long-term enjoyment.


Our team works closely with homeowners to create custom swimming pools that fit their lifestyle and outdoor living goals. Unlike many builders, we focus exclusively on concrete gunite pools and do not offer fiberglass pools, ensuring superior customization and structural strength.
