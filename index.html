About Us

U.S. Pool Builder specializes in premium inground gunite concrete pool construction in Katy, Texas. We focus exclusively on durable, long-lasting pool designs built with superior craftsmanship and attention to detail.


Our team works closely with homeowners from planning to completion, ensuring every project reflects their unique vision. We do not offer fiberglass pools, allowing us to focus entirely on custom gunite pool solutions.
